Easy method to find ores

Finding and mining ores in Minecraft has always been an exciting adventure for most players. We will focus on the new system of ore distribution and generation in this article. Ores of all types generate at different world heights, new biomes, and in unique forms with the latest update. Technically, that’s supposed to make it easier to discover in-game ores. However, it can be a little complex for new (as well as some veteran) players to understand how it works. From finding diamonds to red stones, here’s how the new Minecraft ore distribution works.

Diamond Ore Distribution

Diamond is the most popular ore, and it now only generates below Y=16 world height in Minecraft 1.18.1. And the diamond ore is further maximized at the height of Y= -64 or the bedrock layer.

Emerald Ore Distribution
